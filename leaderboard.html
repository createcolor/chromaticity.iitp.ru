Night Photography Rendering
Challenge 2024

First Leaderboard

For the first initial validation set, each image had multiple renderings, namely the ones submitted by participants. Subsequently, all possible pairs of these renderings were generated and presented to 20 evaluators via the Yander Tasks platform. Their task was to indicate their preference between the images in each pair. This process was iterated for every image in the validation set. The submission's score was determined by the performance of its images in these pairwise evaluations.
